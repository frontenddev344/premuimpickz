Transport

At PremuimPickz, we understand that when you order authentic, signed ice hockey memorabilia, secure and reliable delivery is just as important as authenticity. That’s why we use protective packaging and trusted shipping partners to ensure your collectible arrives in perfect condition.

1

Safe and careful packaging

All signed ice hockey memorabilia:

  • Carefully folded or framed (if applicable)
  • Protected with acid-free materials
  • Securely packaged to prevent damage during shipping

We handle every item with the care it deserves - from our facility to your collection.

2

Delivery locations

We ship worldwide, delivering authentic ice hockey memorabilia to collectors across the UK, Europe, and internationally. Shipping options and costs are clearly displayed at checkout.

3

Processing times

Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days after payment confirmation. Once dispatched, you will receive a shipping confirmation with tracking details to monitor your delivery.

4

Trackable and insured delivery

All orders are shipped using tracked and insured services for complete peace of mind. Delivery times may vary by location, but most UK orders arrive within 2–5 working days.

5

International shipping

International orders may be subject to customs duties, taxes, or import fees, which are the responsibility of the buyer. PremuimPickz is not responsible for delays caused by customs clearance.

6

Reliable delivery

We partner with trusted carriers to ensure your signed hockey memorabilia arrives safely and on time. For any shipping or delivery questions, our support team is always available to help.
