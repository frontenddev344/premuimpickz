Return Policy

At PremuimPickz, we specialize in authentic signed ice hockey memorabilia, including jerseys and collectibles sourced from top players and teams. Every item is carefully reviewed, authenticated, and securely shipped. Please read our return policy before completing your purchase.

Authenticity guarantee

All products offered by PremuimPickz are independently verified and guaranteed to be authentic. Due to the collectible nature of signed ice hockey memorabilia, returns are not accepted for changes of mind or personal preference.

Authorized returns

Returns are only accepted under the following circumstances:

  • The item received differs from its description
  • The item arrives damaged in transit
  • The item is verified as inauthentic (subject to review)

Any issues must be reported within 48 hours of delivery.

Return conditions

To qualify for a return:

  • The item must remain in its original condition
  • All authentication elements (certificates, holograms, tags) must be intact
  • The item must not be worn, displayed, framed, cleaned, or modified

Items that do not meet these requirements will not be eligible for a refund.

Return process

  • Contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order
  • Provide your order number along with clear images of the issue
  • Once approved, we will provide return instructions

Returns sent without prior approval will not be accepted.

Refunds

Refunds are processed after the returned item has been received and inspected. Once approved, funds will be issued to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Shipping Costs

  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the buyer, unless the item is faulty, damaged, or incorrect

International Orders

Customers ordering internationally are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or import fees. These charges are non-refundable.

Questions?

If you need assistance regarding a return or a specific order, feel free to contact us. Our team is always available to help.
